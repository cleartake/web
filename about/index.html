About ClearTake

What ClearTake Is

ClearTake is an independent research project designed to measure how value moves across the AI economy.

Rather than treating AI as a single sector, ClearTake models it as a dynamic value chain — where capital, risk, and momentum shift over time between compute, platforms, and applications. The goal is not prediction, but clarity: making structural patterns visible as they emerge.

What ClearTake Is Not

ClearTake does not provide investment advice. It does not offer stock recommendations, price targets, or buy/sell signals.

The indexes are measurement instruments — lenses for observing structure, concentration, and motion — not instructions for action.

The Philosophy

Markets are stories layered on top of systems.

ClearTake focuses on the system first: structure over narrative, patterns over opinions, measurement over hype.

Indexes are treated as observational tools, not verdicts. They are designed to support thinking, not replace it.

ClearTake is built in public so that definitions, methods, and assumptions can be examined, revised, and improved as understanding deepens.

How to Read the Indexes

A few guiding principles:

  • A high value indicates intensity or concentration — not quality or desirability
  • A rising index signals a strengthening pattern, not a guaranteed continuation
  • A falling index reflects dispersion or rotation, not failure
  • Indexes describe what is happening, not what should be done

Interpretation depends on context, time horizon, and risk tolerance.

Data & Cadence

ClearTake uses publicly available market data and a consistent classification of the AI value chain.

Indexes are updated on a regular cadence (typically weekly) and may be revised as methodology evolves. Transparency and consistency are prioritized over precision theater.

Detailed calculation notes are available in the Methodology section.

Why This Is Public

ClearTake exists to support a broader goal: understanding how AI reshapes economic structure.

By publishing assumptions, limitations, and revisions openly, the project aims to contribute clarity to a space often dominated by speculation.

This is a living system. The indexes evolve as the AI economy evolves.

ClearTake is published by Astuten.io Ltd.
